Team Introduction

Our team, led by Dr. Yang Li, is currently overseeing one project funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China for Young Scientists, one Zhejiang Provincial Basic Public Welfare Research Project, one sub-project of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Key R&D Plan, participating in one national key R&D program, two National Natural Science Foundation projects, and one Zhejiang Provincial Key R&D Plan project. Dr. Yang Li has published more than 20 SCI/EI papers and co-authored one academic monograph. Meanwhile, our team is well-funded, with advanced and comprehensive experimental equipment. Currently, our lab's servers are equipped with 4 RTX 4090 GPUs and 128GB of RAM, providing each student with an individual account, supporting SSH remote connections, high concurrency, and high responsiveness to meet the research and experimental needs of every student.
